Mission

联盟宗旨Our Mission

汇聚自主大模型产业力量，连接技术、场景、人才与生态资源，以开放协作推动联合创新与产业落地，共建可信、可持续的产业生态。Uniting the autonomous LLM industry — connecting technology, scenarios, talent and ecosystem to drive joint innovation and deployment toward a trustworthy, sustainable ecosystem.

Values

共同价值Shared Values

以共同原则连接不同技术路线、机构能力与专业经验。Common principles linking diverse technical paths, institutional capabilities and expertise.

开放中立Open & Neutral

厂商中立、对等参与，不将资格或成果归属于单一技术路线。Vendor-neutral and equal participation across technical paths.

务实协作Pragmatic

围绕真实需求与可验证实践组织协作，避免空泛表态。Collaboration grounded in real needs and verifiable practice.

合规可信Trustworthy

在明确授权与治理边界内共建，保障安全与可信。Building within clear authorisation and governance boundaries.

Mechanism

协作机制How We Collaborate

联盟统筹方向，工作组和专项聚焦议题，成员伙伴根据能力与意愿参与。The alliance sets direction; working groups focus topics; members join by capability and intent.

1
联盟统筹方向Alliance sets direction

确立公开议题与协作原则，管理成果公开边界。Sets public topics, principles and disclosure boundaries.

2
工作组聚焦议题Working groups focus

围绕重点方向组织研究、验证与场景共建。Organise research, validation and scenario co-building.

3
成员按能力参与Members contribute

成员伙伴根据能力与意愿选择合适的参与路径。Members choose paths matching their capability and intent.

Directions

面向长期价值持续行动Acting for long-term value

从共同议题出发，持续形成可参与、可验证、可传播的产业协作。From shared topics to participatory, verifiable, shareable collaboration.

联合研究与验证Joint research & validation

组织跨主体研究与能力验证，沉淀可复用的方法与结论。Cross-entity research and validation into reusable methods.

场景共建与落地Scenario co-building

连接真实业务场景，验证产品化与规模化落地价值。Connecting real scenarios to prove productisation value.
