Responsible Governance

治理边界Governance Boundaries

公开信息以生态协作与治理原则为限，在数据合规与高风险能力治理事项上主动接受有关部门与专家指导。Public information is bounded by governance; compliance and high-risk capabilities follow guidance from authorities and experts.

  • 不披露未经授权的敏感材料与数据；伙伴不被强制交付原始数据。No unauthorised sensitive material; partners are never forced to hand over raw data.
  • 不公开高风险能力或可操作的攻击细节。No high-risk capabilities or actionable attack detail.
  • 数据合规与成果公开需经治理评审与授权确认。Compliance and disclosure require governance review and authorisation.
