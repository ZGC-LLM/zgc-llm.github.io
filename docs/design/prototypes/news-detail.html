联盟网络安全生态专项启动Cybersecurity program launched

联合专业用户、安全企业与科研机构启动网络安全能力验证与场景共建。A joint launch of capability validation and scenario co-building.

发布日期：Published: 2026-07-10

专项背景Background

为推动自主大模型在网络安全领域的能力验证与产业落地，联盟联合专业用户、安全企业、基础模型厂商与科研机构，正式启动网络安全生态专项，坚持厂商中立与合规治理。To advance capability validation and deployment of autonomous LLMs in cybersecurity, the alliance launches a neutral, governed program with professionals, vendors, model providers and research institutions.

六阶段生态闭环The six-stage loop

专项以「需求定义 → 任务设计 → 能力验证 → 专家评测 → 结果反馈 → 产品化落地」形成可追溯、可反馈的持续循环。The program forms a traceable loop: define needs, design tasks, validate, expert review, feedback and productisation.

  • 在合规授权前提下贡献任务集与真实数据。Contribute task sets and compliant data under authorisation.
  • 连接专家评测与真实行业场景。Connect expert review with real scenarios.
  • 成果公开需经治理评审与授权确认。Disclosure requires governance review.

参与方式How to join

机构可通过「生态共建」路径参与，专业用户可通过「专业用户加入」路径参与。Institutions join via Partnership; individuals via the Professional path.
